Features

Centralized Property & Tenant Information

Rentify offers a comprehensive platform for landlords to consolidate all their property and tenant information in one user-friendly interface. This centralization facilitates seamless data management and access, streamlining property-related tasks and contributing to a more efficient property management experience.

AI OCR for Record-Keeping & AI Chatbot Support

Rentify's AI OCR feature makes it easy for landlords to keep track of bills and expenses, ensuring a streamlined financial management process. Our AI-powered chatbot is also available to provide assistance and answer any queries related to the operation of the system, making it easier for landlords and tenants to manage their properties effectively.

Tenant Dashboard for Rent Status and Maintenance Requests

Rentify's property management platform offers a comprehensive tenant dashboard, allowing tenants to monitor their rent status and submit maintenance requests for their apartments. This feature empowers tenants to stay informed and ensures that their concerns are addressed promptly, contributing to a better overall rental experience.
